Our Story

Founded in 2005, GIAT Ltd emerged from a vision to revolutionize industrial automation through precision measurement technologies. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a global leader in level measurement and automation solutions.

Our journey began in London with a simple mission: to provide industries with the most accurate, reliable, and innovative measurement solutions. Today, we serve clients across six continents, delivering cutting-edge technology that drives efficiency and safety in the world's most demanding industrial environments.

From oil refineries in the Middle East to pharmaceutical plants in Europe, from water treatment facilities in Asia to power generation stations in the Americas, GIAT's solutions are trusted by industry leaders worldwide.

Our Journey

2005 - Company Founded

2005

GIAT Ltd was established in London with a team of five engineers, focusing on precision level measurement solutions for the oil and gas industry.

2010 - International Expansion

2010

Expanded operations to the Middle East and Asia, establishing regional offices and partnerships to better serve global clients.

2015 - Technology Innovation

2015

Launched revolutionary laser level transmitter technology, achieving ±2mm accuracy and expanding into pharmaceutical and power generation sectors.

2020 - Digital Transformation

2020

Implemented IoT-enabled solutions and remote monitoring capabilities, enhancing our automation services and customer support.

2025 - Global Leadership

2025

Celebrating 20 years of excellence, serving over 500 clients across 45 countries with cutting-edge automation solutions and unmatched precision.
